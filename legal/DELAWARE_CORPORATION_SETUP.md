## 📋 Delaware Incorporation Process

### Step 1: Name Reservation
**Cost: $75**
- Reserve "Morse-Kirby Technologies Inc." for 120 days
- File with Delaware Division of Corporations
- Ensure name availability and trademark clearance

### Step 2: Articles of Incorporation
**Cost: $89 (standard filing)**
**Required Information:**
- Corporate name and registered agent
- Authorized shares: 10,000,000 common shares
- Par value: $0.001 per share
- Initial directors: 1-3 individuals
- Corporate purpose: General business activities

### Step 3: Registered Agent
**Cost: $200-400/year**
- Delaware registered agent service
- Professional address for legal notices
- Compliance monitoring and reporting

### Step 4: Corporate Bylaws
**Cost: Included with incorporation**
- Board of directors structure
- Shareholder rights and procedures
- Officer appointments and responsibilities
- Meeting requirements and procedures

### Step 5: Initial Board Meeting
**Cost: Legal fees $500-1,500**
- Appoint officers (CEO, CTO, CFO)
- Authorize share issuance
- Approve corporate policies
- Establish banking relationships
